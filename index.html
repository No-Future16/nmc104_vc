Course Policies

This is a hands-on studio course. Active participation is expected as we will have in-class practices that directly impact your bonus points. You can miss up to 9 hours (approx. 3 weeks) over the 14-week semester without formally failing the course.

1 day late: -10% | 2 days late: -20% | 3 days late: -30%. Projects submitted more than 3 days late earn 0 HP (points).

Use AI to ideate, not to do the work. Any AI-generated asset MUST be credited with the exact prompt used. Submitting pure AI work as your own or stealing designs will result in disciplinary action.
