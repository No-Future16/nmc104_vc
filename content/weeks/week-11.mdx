## Generative AI as a Design Tool
Generative AI tools have transformed how designers brainstorm and create assets. However, they are *tools*, not replacements for the designer's eye.

### Prompt Engineering for Designers
Getting good results requires understanding how to talk to the AI. A strong visual prompt usually includes:
1. **Subject:** What is the main focus?
2. **Medium/Style:** Is it a 3D render, a flat vector illustration, a photograph?
3. **Lighting & Perspective:** Dramatic lighting, isometric view, cinematic.
4. **Color Palette:** Neon, pastel, monochrome.

<Alert type="warning">
**Design Tip:** Use AI for heavy lifting (generating complex textures, brainstorming layouts, removing backgrounds) but retain human control over the final typographic hierarchy, UX flow, and emotional delivery.
</Alert>
