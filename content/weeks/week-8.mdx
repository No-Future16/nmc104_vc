## AI-Powered Prototyping
Before diving deep into manual wireframing, we can leverage AI to accelerate the ideation phase. **Stitch with Google** is an experimental tool that allows designers to generate user interface screens simply by writing text prompts.

## The Workflow
1. **Prompting:** Describe the app or screen you want to build (e.g., "A modern e-commerce product landing page with a dark mode aesthetic").
2. **Iteration:** Refine the prompt to adjust layout, typography, and colors.
3. **Export to Figma:** Once the generated screens align with your vision, you can export them directly into Figma. From there, you can adjust the vector layers, change colors, and turn them into interactive prototypes.

<Alert type="info">
**Action Item:** As we prepare for the final portfolio, start thinking about how AI can rapidly generate wireframe structures so you can spend more time on high-fidelity visual polish.
</Alert>
